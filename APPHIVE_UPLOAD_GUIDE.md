### Step 1: Go to Apphive.io

- Click "Sign Up" or "Get Started"

### Step 3: Start New Project
Look for one of these buttons on the homepage:
- **"Create New App"** or

### Step 4: Choose Import Method
You'll see options like:

### Step 5: Upload Your Zip File
1. You'll see an upload area that says "Drop files here or click to browse"
2. Click on it
3. Navigate to: `E:\MY_Projects\Web APP Attendance\`
4. Select: **AttendanceApp-Mobile.zip**
5. Click "Open"

The file will start uploading (should take 10-30 seconds)

---

### Step 6: Configure App Settings
After upload, you'll see a form:

**Fill in these details:**
- **App Name:** Attendance App
- **Package ID:** com.attendance.app (should be auto-filled)
- **Version:** 1.0.0 (should be auto-filled)
- **Description:** Location-based attendance system

**Leave other settings as default**

Click "Continue" or "Next"

---

### Step 7: Choose Platform
Select: **Android** (or both Android & iOS if you want)

---

### Step 8: Build
Click the big **"Build"** or **"Generate APK"** button

---

### Step 9: Wait for Build
- Build process takes **5-15 minutes**
- You'll see a progress bar or status
- You may receive email notification when done

---

### Step 10: Download APK
Once build completes:
1. Click **"Download APK"** button
2. Save the file (usually named: `app-release.apk` or similar)
3. Transfer to your Android phone
